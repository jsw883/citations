Bookings

We are always interested in taking on new events, and our regular events include singing at Mory's, graduate and preofesisonal school such as reunions and holiday parties, and other private events such as birthday parties, cocktail parties, and weddings.

If you are interested in having us sing at your next event then please send us an email at citations@yale.edu or use the form below, and we will get back to you. You can also contact one of our officers directly if you want to ask one of them about something in specific instead.
