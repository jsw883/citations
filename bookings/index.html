Bookings

The Citations are available for campus events, New Haven community functions, and private parties. We are always interested in making new contacts and taking on exciting new events! Our regular performances include singing at Mory's, the traditional Yale dinner club, performing at graduate and professional school events, and numerous private holiday parties leading up to the winter holidays!

If you are interested in having the Citations perform at your next occasion, please send us an email directly to citations@yale.edu or use the form below. We are taking bookings for the rest of the academic year now, so reach out!
