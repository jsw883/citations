The Citations 2014 - 2015

Our members come from departments across the graduate and professional schools. This year, we represent the Graduate School of Arts and Sciences, the School of Medicine, the Law School, and the School of Forestry and Environmental Studies. We pride ourselves on our diverse range of backgrounds, interests, and cultures, which makes for a very close and relaxed group!
