The Citations (2013)

The Citations is Yale Graduate School's answer to what happens when you leave college and think that you have to leave a cappella behind. You don't! With members from many different departments across the graduate and professional schools, such as the Graduate School of Arts and Sciences, the Law School, the School of Management, and the School of Forestry and Environmental Studies, we have an extremely diverse range of backgrounds, interests, and cultures!
