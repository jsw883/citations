Performances

We perform at several events each semester, with more frequent performances around cultural or seasonal holidays. Our regular events include singing at Mory's, a traditional Yale bar and dinner club, graduate and professional school events such as reunions and holiday parties, and other private events such as birthday parties, cocktail parties, and weddings.

We also enjoy performing at business events in New Haven, such as the launch event for the Grove, New Haven's coworking community, and we are always interested in making new contacts and taking on exciting new events!
