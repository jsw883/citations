Yale Founders Day 2014

Yale celebrated the anniversary of the university's creation 313 years ago with our first Founders Day celebration on Oct 14, 2014. The festivities culminated in a Cross Campus celebration with photo booths, seasonal food, and a performance by the Citations during President Salovey's address to the crowd of 2000!

    Spring Concert 2013

    Last year we performed in the common room at the McDougal Graduate Student Center of Arts and Sciences for our end of year Spring concert and got it all on record. Most of our members are still around, but we will miss those who have graduated and moved on to new heights!
